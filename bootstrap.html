No, Its similar like Internship program. Our community members will guide you to learning any technologies as you want to build your career.

What is the duration of the program ?

3 Months, dedicated learning program.

Is it free ?

Yes, its 100% absolutely free. There is no hidden charges for this program at any time or any manner.
